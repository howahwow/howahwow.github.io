About Me

Documenting

Utopias

A Chinese-speaking travel content creator based in the UK. Long-focused on Eastern Europe, the Balkans, the former Yugoslav territories, Soviet architecture, brutalism, urban culture, and historical spaces.

Using images and words to document the places that don't get much attention — but hold enormous power. If you also love architecture, urban wandering, history, and off-the-beaten-path travel, follow along.

All photographs are shot on an iPhone. The imperfect optics, the flat overcast light, the rain — these are features, not bugs. Brutalism was always at its best under grey skies.
